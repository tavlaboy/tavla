Hospitality

Hospitality Inspired by the Silk Road

Georgia's location along the historic Silk Road played a significant role in shaping its culture, where hospitality became a true way of life. For centuries, traders, travellers, and guests passed through the region, and welcoming them with warmth and generosity became a defining part of Georgian identity. In Georgia, hospitality is more than etiquette — it is a deeply rooted value. Guests are treated with genuine respect and care, regardless of their background, culture, or beliefs.

At Tavla, we carry this tradition forward in every detail — from the atmosphere to the service — creating a fine dining experience in Tbilisi where every guest feels truly welcome, valued, and at home. Whether you are visiting for a casual Georgian meal, a special occasion dinner, or simply to discover Tbilisi's Georgian food scene, Tavla is your table.
